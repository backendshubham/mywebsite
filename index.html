Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our repair services.

What types of electrical appliances do you repair?

We repair all major home electrical appliances including refrigerators, washing machines, air conditioners, water heaters, microwave ovens, induction cooktops, and more. If you're unsure about your specific appliance, feel free to contact us.

How much does the home pickup and delivery service cost?

Our home pickup and delivery service is available for a flat charge of ₹149, regardless of the size or type of appliance/device. This fee covers both pickup and return delivery after repairs are completed.

How long do repairs typically take?

Repair times vary depending on the issue and availability of parts. Most common repairs are completed within 2-3 days. For complex issues or if special parts need to be ordered, it may take up to a week. We'll always provide you with an estimated timeline when you bring in your device.

Do you provide warranty on repairs?

Yes, we provide a 7-day warranty on all repairs and replaced parts. If the same issue reoccurs within this period, we'll fix it at no additional cost (excluding physical damage or new issues).

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, UPI payments (PhonePe, Google Pay, PayTM), and debit/credit cards. Payment is due when you collect your repaired device or when we deliver it back to you.
